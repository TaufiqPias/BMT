Welcome to the Bid Maker Tool - For Property Preservation and Rehab


This tool is developped to help property preservation processors to work faster and with percision. I hope it helps to you all.The bid inventory will be updated continuously. If you are unable to find a bid in the inventory please feel free to email me at taufiq.xenox@gmail.com.

BENEFITS

Faster Bid Preparing ♦ Low Chance Of Error ♦ All Bids In One Place ♦ Easy To Use ♦ Input Data Once & Make Multiple Bids

HOW TO USE

  1. Search the Categories for your services.
  2. Go to the page and fill out the form.
  3. Generate as many BID as you like.
  4. Make sure to copy all the bids (or individual bids) on a Notepad/Word.
  5. Enjoy

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to me.
